# ADR Template

* Status: proposed / accepted / deprecated
* Deciders: <names>
* Date: YYYY-MM-DD
* Technical story: <short story or ticket reference>

## Context and Problem Statement

Describe the issue or context that requires a decision. Keep it concise and include any constraints.

## Considered Options

List the options that were considered (one per bullet).

## Decision outcome

State the chosen option and a short justification for the decision.

## Consequences

List the positive and negative consequences of the decision. Include operational, testing, and maintenance impacts.

## Alternatives considered

Short notes on alternatives and why they were not chosen.

## Links

References, related docs, tickets, or design notes.
